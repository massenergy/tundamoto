About Us

If we define poverty as a disease, then there must be cures for it. Tundamoto serves as both a platform and a project, acting as a cure for poverty.

Tundamoto is a five-sided platform that enables diverse stakeholders to engage in an open, transparent, and auditable manner to implement solutions for poverty.

The Tundamoto project aims to create a new smart cash crop for sub-Saharan countries by planting 2 billion dragon fruit plants across 1 million acres, owned by 1 million different farmers. It will also implement 3,500 collection centers and nurseries, supported by 3,500 water distribution networks, each with a borehole, and 350 factories for value-added processing of dragon fruit.

This new cash crop initiative will be launched in three sub-Saharan countries.

Expected Outcomes

If we do our work well, then we expect to impact directly the following 10 UN SDGs

Ending Poverty

A decent guaranteed income at the honestead level for land that the homesteads already own is a game changer.

Zero Hunger

This has ripple effects from health, to education, to human productivity.

Good Health and Well-Being

Poverty and poor health go hand in hand. No more.

Quality Education

Income at the homestead level guarantees children will go the school or university they merit.

Gender Equality

Income at the homestead level liberates women from chains brought about by poverty.

Clean Water and Sanitation

Income at the homestead level ensures there is money to pay for utilities like sewer lines, piped water and power.

Affordable and Clean Energy

Penetartion of utilities is very low in sub-Sahara Africa becuase there is no guaranteed income at the household level. Tundamoto will cahnge that.

Decent Work and Economic Growth

A cash crop for arid and semi-arid lands will unlock alot of economic growth and provide some decent and diginified work.

Reduced Inequality

The economic prospects of a homestead or a region with a cash crop and those without is like the difference between nightime and daytime.

Cliamte Action

Dragon fruit is perfect for climate change, since it needs little rain and its CAM photosynthesis is perfect for rising temperatures.

Innovative partnerships

Tundamoto project can only be dreamt of and actualized through getting into very innovative partnerships that are founded on transparent auditable systems.
